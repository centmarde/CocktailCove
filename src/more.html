About the App

Welcome to Cocktail Cove, Our app provides everything you need to explore and enjoy the world of cocktails from the comfort of your own home. From classic favorites to innovative creations, join us at Cocktail Cove to discover, create, and savor the perfect drink for every occasion. Cheers to unforgettable moments and unforgettable flavors!

Services

Cocktail Cove offers a range of services tailored to enhance your cocktail experience.

Create and Customize Your Cocktails: Experiment with ingredients and quantities to craft personalized cocktails that suit your taste preferences.

Creators

Cocktail Cove was created as a school project for our IT class by Jenny, Kyte, and Zoey. Combining our passion for mixology and programming, we developed an app that provides cocktail recipes and fosters a sense of community among enthusiasts. Our goal is to make mixology accessible to everyone, from seasoned bartenders to curious beginners, and we hope you enjoy using Cocktail Cove!
